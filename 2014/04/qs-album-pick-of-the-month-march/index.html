Q's album pick of the month: March

Apr 7, 2014 · 1 minute read
Album PicksMusic

Welcome to the first post of the Q’s album pick of the month series! Without further ado, the album that I pick for March is:

Distal by Crash of Rhinos (2011)

This album has been playing over and over again in my car. It’s my daily soundtrack during my commute to college. Great riffs, great drumming and tremendous energy from the first to the last track. It’s it an interesting blend of punk, post-rock, hardcore and math-rock.

You can get it for free (or maybe give these guys some support!) at bandcamp: http://crashofrhinos.bandcamp.com/album/distal
